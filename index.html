Cats: My Favorite Animal

A lifelong journey of companionship

How Cats Have Shaped My Life

Cats have been a part of my entire life. Since childhood, I've always had at least two cats, so most of my attachments come from my earliest memories of cuddling and caring for them. While my sister would stay home to babysit me, she'd always be busy doing her school work, so my cats always kept me company.

Why People Love Cats

  • Quiet companionship
  • Subtle affection
  • Unique personalities
  • Comforting presence during ordinary and difficult moments
  • Independence combined with chosen closeness
  • Softness, purring, and calm beauty
  • Simple but powerful joy in everyday life
